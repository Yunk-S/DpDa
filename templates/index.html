Three Major Diseases

Stroke
Second leading cause of death globally

Stroke is caused by a sudden interruption or rupture of blood supply to the brain, accounting for approximately 11% of total global deaths.

Risk factors: Hypertension, diabetes, smoking, age, unhealthy diet, etc.

Predict Stroke Risk
Heart Disease
Leading cause of death globally

Cardiovascular diseases claim 17.9 million lives annually, accounting for 31% of global deaths.

Risk factors: Hypertension, hyperlipidemia, diabetes, smoking, obesity, etc.

Predict Heart Disease Risk
Cirrhosis
Late-stage manifestation of liver disease

Cirrhosis is the late stage of scarring (fibrosis) of the liver caused by various forms of liver disease and conditions.

Risk factors: Long-term heavy drinking, viral hepatitis, non-alcoholic fatty liver disease, etc.
