Multi-Disease Risk Prediction Model Description

This multi-disease risk prediction model uses advanced machine learning technology. By analyzing common features and individual features of three diseases, it establishes a multi-dimensional risk assessment system. Main features include:

  • Disease Correlation Analysis: Considers the interactions and comorbidity mechanisms between stroke, heart disease, and cirrhosis
  • Comprehensive Risk Score: Calculates single disease risk as well as combined risk for multiple disease combinations
  • Data-Driven Decision: Provides data-supported risk prediction based on large amounts of clinical data training
  • Personalized Prediction: Provides customized risk assessment based on individual health characteristics

The model uses a Bayesian probability framework, combining conditional probability and correlation analysis to calculate risk probabilities for different disease combinations, providing users with a comprehensive health risk profile.
